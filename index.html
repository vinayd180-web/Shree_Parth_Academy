About Us

Director's Desk

Mr. Swapnil Kalaskar

With over 15 years of experience in education, our director envisions a learning environment where every child discovers their true potential. He believes in holistic development that goes beyond textbooks, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and moral values.

"We are not competitors; we are sculptors of future leaders."

Our Mission

To Enable The Students Success Beyond Excellence In Their Life Long Pursuit Of Knowledge And In Their Contribution As Responsible Citizens In A Rapidly Changing Globalized World. We aim to provide quality education that empowers students to excel academically and personally.


Our Vision

To Become A Standard Of Excellence Fostering Intellect, Creativity And Character In An Active, Student Centered Learning Community. We envision a future where every student is a lifelong learner and a responsible global citizen.


Our Motive

"In Every Child There Is A Hidden Warrior. It Only Requires Self Confidence And Proper Guidance." Our motive is to ignite that inner warrior and guide each child towards their unique path of success.

Our Courses

Academic Programs

  • 5th to 10th English Medium (State Board)
    Comprehensive coverage of all subjects with regular assessments.
  • CBSE (5th to 10th)
    Focused preparation for CBSE curriculum with expert guidance.
  • 11th & 12th Commerce (State Board & CBSE)
    In-depth study of Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and more.
  • Foundation Courses (8th & 9th)
    Strong foundation building for future competitive exams.
  • Competitive Exam Preparation (JEE, NEET, MHT‑CET)
    Specialized coaching for medical and engineering entrance exams.
  • Spoken English & Personality Development
    Enhance communication skills and overall personality.
  • Abacus & Vedic Maths
    Mental maths and speed calculation techniques.
  • Coding & Robotics
    Hands-on learning in programming and robotics for young innovators.

Each course is designed with a focus on conceptual clarity, regular assessments, and individual attention. We also conduct periodic parent-teacher meetings to track progress.
